Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register on Raktamitra?

Anyone willing to donate blood and meet the standard eligibility criteria for blood donation (e.g., age, weight, and health requirements) can register as a donor. Blood recipients or their family members can also register to seek assistance.

Is Raktamitra free to use?

Yes, Raktamitra is a free platform designed to make blood donation accessible and hassle-free for everyone.

How can I register as a donor?

You can register as a donor by signing up on the platform, providing your details (name, blood type, location, and contact information).

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, Raktamitra values your privacy. Your personal information is only shared with recipients in genuine need of blood.

How will I be contacted if my blood is needed?

If your blood type matches a recipient's requirements, you will be contacted by the recipients via call or message.

Are there any eligibility criteria to donate blood?

Yes, to donate blood, you must:

  • Be between 18 and 65 years old.
  • Weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • Be in good health and not on any medication that prevents donation.
  • Have a minimum hemoglobin level of 12.5 g/dL.

How often can I donate blood?

Men can donate blood every 3 months, while women can donate every 4 months, provided they meet all other eligibility requirements.

How do I request blood on Raktamitra?

To request blood, you can register as a recipient and submit a blood request form. Include details such as the required blood group, location, and urgency of the need.

How quickly can I find a donor?

The time to find a donor depends on the availability of compatible donors in your area and the urgency of your request.

What blood types can I request?

You can request any blood type (e.g., A, B, AB, O, and Rh factors). The platform will match you with donors who have the required blood type.

How do I update my donor or recipient profile?

Log in to your account, navigate to the profile section, and update your details. Make sure to save the changes before exiting.

What should I do if I face issues with the platform?

If you encounter technical difficulties, contact our support team via email or the help section. Provide a detailed description of the issue to receive prompt assistance.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, donating blood is a safe procedure. Sterile equipment is used, and the process is conducted by trained professionals.

Are there any risks involved in receiving donated blood?

Blood transfusions are generally safe, but recipients should ensure the transfusion is conducted by a certified medical facility.

About Raktamitra

Raktamitra is a platform dedicated to bridging the gap between blood donors and recipients. Our mission is to ensure that no one has to wait for life-saving blood in times of need. Join us and be a part of this noble cause to save lives.
